President, FBCCI

Md. Jashim Uddin is, a business graduate, leading business personality, an industrialist and a philanthropist with a vision to develop the livelihood of the people of Bangladesh by providing income generating opportunities through making them skilled manpower to reduce poverty, adding value to the economy and protecting the environment. His focus is developing green and environmentally friendly business practices and support sick industries through leadership and govt. involvement.
